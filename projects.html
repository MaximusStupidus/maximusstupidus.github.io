Projects

A few things I've built, across voice AI, aerospace, optics, and controls.

Comsync

AI-native voice automation for enterprise sales and support. Production voice agents over a full STT → LLM → TTS pipeline, with interruption handling and turn-taking, plus an observability layer that captures per-utterance latency, transcript diffs, and sentiment for live calls.

Monte Carlo Flight Simulator

A headless flight simulator for LAT Aerospace that runs ~10,000 parallel cases on AWS EC2 via Docker, cutting iteration time from six months to two days. Paired with a 6-DOF control law for Upper Surface Blowing, validated in hardware-in-the-loop testing with ArduPilot.

VibroView

Phase-based motion amplification (Laplacian & Riesz pyramids) that reveals micro-vibrations to predict machine health. Tracks centroid displacement to 50 microns at 99% frequency accuracy; field-tested at a Tata Power thermal plant and validated against 16,500 fps cameras.

3D Glass Marking with Femtosecond Lasers

Software that slices STL models into 2D layers and emits G-code to engrave 3D structures inside glass with a femtosecond laser, with a DFS path planner to minimize redundant scans.

Femtosecond Pulse Measurement (FROG)

A lower-cost temporal-profile measurement rig for femtosecond pulses, reducing mirror count versus commercial auto-correlators. Built in the Vibrational Spectroscopy Lab under Prof. Dipanshu Bansal, with working FROG and auto-correlator prototypes now being miniaturised toward a product for the Indian ultrafast-optics market. Validated by RRCAT and funded by SINE, IITB.

Quadrotor Control — Team AeRoVe

Robust fixed-time sliding-mode control for quadrotor trajectory tracking, with time-optimal min-snap trajectories solved via OSQP, tested on the IRIS drone in Gazebo through MAVROS.

Nifty 50 Stock Screener

A modular screener that runs exploratory analysis on the Nifty 50 and forecasts prices with a deep-learning model (~57% accuracy), alongside a frontend surfacing per-stock metrics like ROE, ROCE, and ROI — built toward an industry-standard screening tool with price prediction baked in.

Agrover

An autonomous agricultural rover (a play on "agriculture" + "rover") that identifies crop disease and does targeted, guided pesticide spraying, cutting the manual spraying that harms farmers' health. Built as IIT Bombay's Institute Technical Summer Project.

S.T.A.R. — UMIC, IIT Bombay

An autonomous robot that navigates arenas and handles ball detection, obstacle avoidance, and path-finding, built on Python (NumPy, OpenCV), ROS (Gazebo, RVIZ), SolidWorks, and Ubuntu.

Analysis of Non-Circular Members under Torsional Loading

An FEM framework for torsional stress and deformation in non-circular cross-sections — square, elliptical, and airfoil geometries — validated against St. Venant's torsion theory using Prandtl stress functions. Written for ME202 at IIT Bombay.
